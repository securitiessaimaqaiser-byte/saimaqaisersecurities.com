Market risk refers to the possibility of losses due to changes in market prices, interest rates, or overall market sentiment. Securities traded on Pakistan Stock Exchange may be affected by volatility and liquidity constraints.

Liquidity Risk

Some securities may not be readily tradable in the market due to low trading volumes. This may affect the ability to buy or sell securities at desired prices.
