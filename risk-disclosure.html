Risk Disclosure

Understanding the risks associated with investing in capital markets

General Investment Risk

Investment in securities, commodities, and capital market instruments involves risk. Market conditions, economic factors, and political developments may affect the value of investments and returns.

Market Volatility

Prices of securities traded on the Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX) may fluctuate significantly due to market demand, supply, investor sentiment, and external economic factors. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

Liquidity Risk

Certain securities may have limited liquidity, making it difficult to buy or sell positions at desired prices or within a desired timeframe.

Regulatory & Compliance Risk

Capital market activities are subject to regulations by authorities including the Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP), Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX), Central Depository Company (CDC), and National Clearing Company of Pakistan Limited (NCCPL). Regulatory changes may impact market operations and investment outcomes.

Technology & System Risk

Trading activities rely on electronic systems and communication networks. System failures, connectivity issues, or cyber incidents may disrupt trading or result in financial loss.
